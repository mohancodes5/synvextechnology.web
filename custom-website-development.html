Custom Website Development

We design and build custom websites that are fast, accessible, and optimized for search engines.

What we deliver

  • Requirement analysis & content strategy focused on target keywords and user journeys.
  • Performance-first front-end using modern HTML, efficient CSS, and lightweight JavaScript.
  • Mobile-first responsive layouts and accessibility (WCAG) best practices.
  • SEO fundamentals: semantic markup, title/meta optimization, structured data (Schema.org), and crawlable architecture.
  • Conversion optimizations: clear CTAs, A/B testing hooks, and analytics event wiring.

Recommended process

  1. Discovery & keyword mapping
  2. Prototyping & content structure
  3. Development (performance budgets & image optimization)
  4. On-page SEO and structured data implementation
  5. Testing, launch, and 30-day monitoring

Typical timeline & pricing

Landing pages: 1–2 weeks. Small brochure site (5–10 pages): 3–4 weeks. Custom feature-rich sites: timeline and pricing quoted after discovery. We provide fixed-price proposals for clearly scoped projects and milestone billing for larger builds.
